On the Origin of Religion

The beginnings of organized Religion 
It is certain that spirituality existed well before organized religion did. from the moment the humans started burying their dead coma we can be sure that the thought about the concept of afterlife, and their place within reality, Or more precisely the place of Consciousness within the continuous nature of time, and the possibility of Consciousness existing Beyond the physical life. These realizations would have  inevitably  lead to the conclusion that the universe was created, and if it was created, that its creator  had set in place a way for consciousness to be eternal.
